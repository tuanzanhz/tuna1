Payment option

1.Payment in cash?
As a traditional and popular form of payment in Vietnam for many generations, cash payment method is used at most business locations. At many media store locations, this is the only payment solution adopted by many store owners. The advantage of this form is mainly suitable for small and retail clothing stores in areas with sparse customers. Shop owners will have money right after the purchase and sale transaction without incurring transaction fees, without going through an intermediary device. At the same time, there is no need to invest in payment equipment.

2.Pay by receiving payment when shopping online?
 Many shops that apply the online clothing store business form often apply the payment method ship cod - Delivery on behalf of. This method can simply be understood as the parties through a 3rd party carrier to deliver the goods to the customer. This payment method allows store owners to receive orders from shipping carriers. The customer will then pay this party.

3.Payment by bank transfer?
 The person who runs a clothing store for remote customers wants to pay the shop owner completely without going through a third party or paying by installments in small installments. In addition, when customers buy at the store without cash and POS card machines, this will be a very reasonable solution. However, this form also has some limitations such as:
- Time to receive money to the account is late compared to the sending time
- Loss of transfer fee
- The minimum payment limit depends on the bank and may encounter errors and transaction errors.
