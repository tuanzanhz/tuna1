How can I return a product I purchased online?
Returns may be made by mail or in store. The return window for online purchases is 30 days (10 days in the case of beauty items) from the date of delivery. You may return items by selecting "Return this Item" from your my Madam account under order details, through your delivery confirmation email or by contacting a Client Advisor. Once the request has been approved, your prepaid shipping label will be emailed to you or will be available for download in your my Madam account.
What is your online return policy? How many days do I have to return a product?
Extended returns for the holiday season. Orders placed from November 14th to December 8th will include complimentary 45 day extended returns. Eligible returns of products purchased on Madam.com may be made by mail or in store. Items must be returned in accordance with the requirements outlined below, within 30 days (10 days in the case of beauty products) from the date of delivery to the Madam return address listed on the return shipping label or to a Madam store in the United States (excluding Madam outlet stores). Please note, corporate gifts must be returned exclusively to Madam.com.
How do I exchange an item I purchased online?
We are happy to facilitate exchanges for online purchases. To request an exchange, please contact our Client Advisors at +1.877.482.2430. Returned merchandise must meet the conditions and guidelines listed in the Online Return Policy in order to be accepted for an exchange. Please note exchanges are subject to merchandise availability.
Can I exchange or return an item gifted to me by someone else?
Gift items purchased on Madam.com may be returned. However, refunds may only be issued to the same credit card used for original payment. Orders purchased through the 4Gift platform will be refunded by 4Gift to the gift contributors, not the gift recipient.

At this time, Madam.com cannot offer exchanges for returned merchandise. We suggest you return the item you wish to exchange for a refund using the applicable conditions and guidelines above and place a new order on Madam.com or with one of our Client Advisors. Please note new orders are subject to merchandise availability.

For items received through 4Gift, the gift recipient will have the option of exchanging their gift before finalizing the order. Giftees may select a new color variation of the item or a different item with the same value, if available on Madam.com. They will have the opportunity to select their preferred size for sizeable items, such as ready-to-wear.

Once the gift recipient has confirmed the selection, the order will be finalized and no exchanges can be made.

Please refer to our Online Return Policy for more information and return instructions.
